Frequently Asked Questions:

What makes Tokn Music different?
Tokn Music is building the worlds best NFT marketplace that provides investors real intrinsic value without the gas prices or crypto confusion.
What do artists get?
Artists can earn upfront capital and passive income from their streaming royalties without forfeiting copyrights, control, and creativity.
What do investors get?
Investors purchase a percentage of streaming royalties through tokens which can be sold at an appreciated price.
How do I buy tokens?
Right now, tokens are being listed and sold on Opensea until the launch of our marketplace.
What are tokens?
Each token holds a percentage of a streaming royalties and be exchanged between investors. Additionally, tokens are a ticket to launch - if you are holding a token at launch, you get early access.
When do investors receive royalties?
Investors receive royalties at our launch date. Whoever holds a token at launch will receive the lump sum of royalties earned from that designated token.
